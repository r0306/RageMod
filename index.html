RageMod for Bukkit

RageMod is a Bukkit plugin, designed to turn Minecraft into something new on all servers that use it, it implements:

NPCs (with the Citizens API). NPC Towns (ran by players, populated by NPCs). Player Towns (ran and populated by players). Quests (an extensive questing system, quests can be reserved, and some repeated). A capitol city (with a huge lot system, economy done by Vault). Permissions for almost everything (requires Vault). Other Misc. Features (e.g compass commands and temporary building permits for the capitol).
